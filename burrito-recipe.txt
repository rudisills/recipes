1 Burrito skin
1/4 Can refried beans or chili
1/4 cup cheese
Salsa or hot sauce packets to taste

Directions:
1. Place the burrito skin on a plate
2. Spread the refried beans/chili and cheese in a line like a hot dog.
3. Cover completely with a paper towel.
4. Microwave for 45 seconds.
5. Remove from microwave and add salsa/hot sauce to taste.
6. Enjoy! (Be careful to avoid burning yourself if the cheese/beans are too hot).
